Characters

There are 62 Characters available. 5 are unlocked by default, the others are unlocked through Progress.

Each character has their own Stats and starting Weapons. Many have unique mechanics, and a handful also start with certain Items.

Most characters also use Item Tags, which gives more weight to items that match their playstyle.
